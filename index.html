About Us

We are OUTFITTERY, Europe's biggest personal shopping service for men.

We are OUTFITTERY, Europe's biggest personal shopping service for men.

To use our service, you first answer a few simple questions about your style, sizes and price preferences. Your stylist will then select the perfect pieces of clothing for your needs and wardrobe - whether that's outfits or specific items. Your stylist can choose from a range of around 100 brands and will send your selection to your home for you to try on. You only keep the items you like, and send the rest back to us, free of any service or shipping charges. OUTFITTERY was founded in Berlin in 2012. In May 2019, the company merged with the Curated Shopping Group (Modomoto, among other brands).
